Game Description and Gameplay

Description

It is a multiplayer game in which two players compete against each other by killing and evading space enemies. 
When the attacker (Enemy) collides with the player, they give different damage, as they have bullets and varying hit damage. 
We offer a health booster for players so they can replenish their health that has been depleted by the attackers.

Gameplay

-Two player game
-Player Navigate left and right to avoid enemy 
-Avoid attacker & their bullets 
-Health Booster to replenish the decreased health
-Whoever Dies first, loses.

Game Logic and rules

-The Players can move left, right, a, &, d to avoid attackers.
-Players can shoot enemy down by left, right, a, & d key.
-The enemies speed is randomized, so they will challenge you at a different speed each time.
-When the Attackers hit the players, they deal various damage.
-Enemies randomly spawn from the top with random speed.
-After a specific amount of time has passed, a health booster will spawn from the top.
-Health booster can fill up the depleted health of players.
-The loser is assumed to be the one who dies first.
-You can check Guide on main menu to see the controls.
-Three audio tracks: one for the backdrop, one for shooting the opponents, and one for the enemy burst
